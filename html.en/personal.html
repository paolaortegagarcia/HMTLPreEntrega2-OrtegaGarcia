Paola Ortega García

I was born on October 4, 1995 in Bogota, Colombia. I am currently 27 years old!

Since I was very young I was passionate about the artistic world, I began to sing and play the piano and guitar. It's something that I still do nowadays.

I decided to emigrate to Buenos Aires, the city where I currently live, to continue doing what I was so passionate about. I had the opportunity to pursue a career in music production and accompany my studies with dance and acting classes. This helped me a lot to become the person I am now.

I also worked in customer service, which helped me a lot to relate to people and understand every need to make a business a success. I've been able to mix both customer service and my love for music and shows by working at some events.

Due to the pandemic, I discovered the magic of computer, since school I was good at all the technological stuff but I never paid too much attention to it. I started with some Data Analyst classes and each time my interest in this area was awakened even more. I decided to continue and look for more courses that involved coding. I supplemented my studies with excel, a tool I use in my work, and I am currently pursuing a career in Full Stack.

Although they are totally different areas, from these disciplines I have been able to learn what is commitment, dedication, attention and reasoning. It is something I am grateful for every day and has helped me grow personally and professionally.
